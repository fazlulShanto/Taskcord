| Project Type              | Best For                                                       | Key Features Used                                                  |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Software Development**  | Agile teams, sprints, bug tracking                             | Kanban boards, labels for priority, custom fields for story points |
| **Content Production**    | Editorial calendars, blog management, video production         | Calendar view, due dates, checklists for publishing workflow       |
| **Marketing & Campaigns** | Campaign planning, social media scheduling, event coordination | Timeline view, labels for channels, attachments for assets         |
| **HR & Recruitment**      | Hiring pipelines, onboarding processes                         | Checklists, template cards, due dates for milestones               |
| **Sales & CRM**           | Lead tracking, deal pipelines, customer onboarding             | Custom fields for deal value, labels for stages                    |
| **Operations & IT**       | Help desk tickets, equipment requests, maintenance             | Automation (Butler), forms for intake, labels for urgency          |
| **Event Planning**        | Conferences, weddings, corporate events                        | Checklists, due dates, attachments for vendor contracts            |
| **Education & Research**  | Course planning, thesis management, research projects          | Labels for subjects, checklists for modules                        |
| **Personal Productivity** | Goal tracking, habit building, life admin                      | Simple lists, labels for categories, due dates                     |
